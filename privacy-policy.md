DotPoet ("we", "the app") is a poetry writing and community app. This policy explains
what we collect, why, and your choices.

## What we collect

- **Account email** — to create and secure your account.
- **Profile details you choose to add** — display name, username, bio, avatar image.
- **Content you publish** — poems, comments, and related activity (likes, bookmarks,
  follows).
- **A user identifier** — to link the above to your account.

Drafts you write stay **on your device** (and, if you enable it, sync privately to your
own iCloud). They are not visible to us or other users unless you publish them.

## How we use it

We use this data only to **run the service** — authenticate you, show your published
work to other members, and operate community features. We do **not** sell your data and
we do **not** use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

## Sign-in providers

If you sign in with **Apple, Google, or Facebook**, we receive a verification token and
basic profile information (such as your email and name) solely to create your account.
We use Facebook **Limited Login** with advertising/analytics features disabled.

## Where your data lives

Account and published content are stored on our backend provider, **Supabase**.
Connections use HTTPS.

## Visibility

Published poems, your username, display name, avatar, and bio are visible to other
members. Drafts remain private to your device unless published.

## Your choices

- Edit your profile at any time in **Settings → Edit Profile**.
- **Delete your account** at any time in **Settings → Delete Account**; this removes
  your account and associated content.
- Block or report other members from their profile / the post menu.

## Children

DotPoet is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from
them.

## Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new date.
